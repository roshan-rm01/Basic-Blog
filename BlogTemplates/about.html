About Me

By roshan-rm01

I am currently a Computer Science student at Queen Mary, University of London in the UK. My studies consist of learning cryptographic algorithms such as the hash algorithm in Java, programming in assembly language and learning about the computer architecture. I also have a Chartered Institute of Securities and Investments qualification.

My most proficient programming languages are Python and Java while I am also proficient in Django web development (Back-end). Other programming languages I am familiar with are HTML/CSS, PHP, SQL and JavaScript.

For my projects, I developed a Monte Carlo Simulation to model the pricing of a call option using Python and I also developed an Automation Tool for MS Excel. In Java, I developed a Stock Market Simulator and a word search solver. My web projects consists of developing a simple investor-start-up match maker, an eCommerce site and a simple social image sharing site.
